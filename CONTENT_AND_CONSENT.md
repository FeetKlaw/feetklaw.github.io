# Content, Consent & Performer Compliance Policy
**Last Updated: February 4, 2026**

Feet Klaw is committed to operating under strict ethical, legal, and
professional standards for adult content production, presentation,
and community management.

This policy governs all content associated with Feet Klaw.

---

## 1. Age Verification (18 U.S.C. §2257 Compliance)

All individuals depicted in any content associated with Feet Klaw:

- Are verified to be **at least eighteen (18) years of age**
- Were 18+ **at the time of content creation**
- Have their age verification records maintained in accordance with
  applicable laws and industry standards

Feet Klaw maintains reasonable compliance practices consistent with
18 U.S.C. §2257 and related record-keeping requirements, where applicable.

---

## 2. Informed & Voluntary Consent

All content is created and presented under the following conditions:

- Participation is **fully voluntary**
- Consent is **informed, explicit, and documented**
- Participants understand the nature, scope, and intended audience
  of the content
- No coercion, pressure, manipulation, or exploitation is permitted

Consent may be withdrawn prospectively, subject to applicable agreements
and legal limitations.

---

## 3. Prohibited Content (Zero Tolerance)

Feet Klaw strictly prohibits, without exception:

- Minors or individuals under 18
- Age-play or simulated underage themes
- Non-consensual acts or representations
- Exploitation, abuse, or coercion
- Human trafficking or forced participation
- Misrepresentation of age or identity
- Illegal activities of any kind

Any violation results in immediate removal and potential legal action.

---

## 4. Performer Rights & Dignity

Feet Klaw respects performer dignity and personal rights.

- Performers retain personal rights as defined by law
- Content ownership and usage rights are governed by written agreements
- Feet Klaw does not permit harassment, doxxing, or exploitation of performers

Harassment or misuse of performer likeness may result in access termination
and legal enforcement.

These actions may be taken without notice and without liability.
